Our Aim

The lack of green spaces significantly worsens environmental problems such as poor air quality, higher temperatures, and increased stress among people.

Our aim is to explore how green spaces contribute to healthier and more sustainable communities.

We also want to raise awareness about increasing green areas in urban environments by using smart technology to improve and manage green spaces effectively.
