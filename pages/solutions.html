Save Green Spaces

Green spaces help reduce pollution, improve mental health, provide fresh air, and support wildlife in urban environments.

SOLUTIONS

Increase tree planting with the help of technology, protect forests, create public parks, and encourage sustainable smart development to maintain healthy green environments.

IoT Soil Moisture Sensors

Smart soil sensors monitor moisture levels in real time, helping plants receive the right amount of water while reducing unnecessary water waste.

Drone Monitoring

Drone monitoring uses drones to observe and collect data from parks, forests, and urban areas. It helps track environmental changes, monitor green spaces, and quickly identify problems such as pollution or illegal deforestation.

Hydroponic Farming

Hydroponic Farming is a way of growing plants without using any soil. Instead, plants grow in water with nutrients. It saves water, space, and allows year-round farming. It is often used in places where soil is poor, land is limited, or people want faster and cleaner crop production.
